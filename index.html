Knights & Knaves

A cozy logic quest. On this island, Knights always tell the truth and Knaves always lie. Reason out who's who — the cleaner your deductions, the more points you earn.

How to play

🛡️
Knights only ever say true things.
🎭
Knaves only ever say false things.
🕵️
A Spy (later levels) can do either — truth or lie.
🎯
Tag everyone, then Check. The fewer mistakes, the more points.
